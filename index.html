How to play

Guess the hidden word before you run out of tries.

Each row is one guess. The mode chip shows the current layout, like 7x5 for seven letters and five tries.

Use the on-screen keyboard or your physical keyboard. Press Enter to submit and Back to erase.

Watch the tile colors after each guess, then narrow down the next attempt.

The hint button can be used once per round and locks after you close the help window.
