How to play

Guess the hidden word before you run out of tries.

Each row is one guess.

The mode chip shows the current layout, like 7x5 for seven letters and five tries.

Watch the tile colors after each guess to guide the next one.

The hint can be used once per round.

Controls

Tap or type letters to fill the row.

Enter submits a full row.

Back removes the last letter.

Hold Back to clear the current row.

New Word starts another round.

Help opens the guide and hint.

Settings controls sound and desktop keyboard display.
